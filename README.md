Data cited in the appendix slide of `index.html` is drawn from:

- Gallup, *Confidence in Institutions* (annual time series): <https://news.gallup.com/poll/1597/confidence-institutions.aspx>

The CSV extracts in `gallup_data/` correspond to:

- `data-9gYgM.csv` — Higher education (2015, 2018, 2023–2025)
- `data-qRWE2.csv` — Police (1993–2025)
- `data-MhYNr.csv` — Public schools (1973–2025)

Figures plotted are the share of U.S. adults expressing "a great deal" or "quite a lot" of confidence in each institution.
